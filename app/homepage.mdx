## NextNotes

A modern full-stack blog reader built with Next.js and Turbopack. Save articles, track your reading, and discover great content.

[Browse blogs](/blogs "Explore all blogs")

---

**Track your reading.** Every blog you add lands in your personal reading list. Mark them as read as you go.

**Your API, your data.** Generate a personal token and access your information from anywhere.

**Built with care.** Drizzle ORM, PostgreSQL, Tailwind CSS v4, and React 19.

---

### What you can do

**Curate your list** — Add blogs from around the web and build a reading list that matters to you.

**Stay organized** — Unread and read sections help you keep track of where you left off.

**Go at your own pace** — No notifications, no pressure. Just a clean space for the content you choose.
